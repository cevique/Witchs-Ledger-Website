What's Inside

Built for Serious Students

Everything you need to conquer entry tests, nothing you don't.

🎯
Smart Goals
Set daily targets for MCQs, study hours, review sessions, and past papers — both globally and per subject. Fully customizable, fully yours.
⏱️
Pomodoro Timer
Built-in Pomodoro timer that auto-logs your study hours on completion. No manual entry — the witch and the sorcerer count your effort for you.
🔥
Streak Tracking
Maintain your daily study streak and watch your 14-day history unfold on the Dashboard. The chain must not be broken.
📚
Board Exam Support
Full FBISE coverage — Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, ICS (Phy & Stats) with all compulsory subjects included automatically.
🌙
Umineko Theme
Golden amber, deep crimson, midnight blue, regal purple. Dark/light mode with OS preference detection. Study in style.
💾
Local-First
All your data lives on your device. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking. Built with TanStack Router and React 19 for a snappy experience.
